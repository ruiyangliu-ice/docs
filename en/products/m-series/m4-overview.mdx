The M4 Series uses iToF RGB-D imaging to output depth, RGB, and amplitude maps in sync. It includes the M4 Mega, M4, and M4 Pro for indoor and outdoor object recognition, localization, measurement, and robot integration.

<Info>
Download complete product materials from the [Download center](/en/resources/downloads/specifications-and-user-guides).
</Info>

## Choose a model

| Model | Best suited for | Typical interfaces and protection |
| --- | --- | --- |
| M4 Mega | Higher frame rates, global-shutter RGB, and four I/O channels | Gigabit Ethernet, CAN, four I/O channels; IP67 |
| M4 | General-purpose RGB-D perception | Gigabit Ethernet, CAN; IP42 |
| M4 Pro | Integrations that require RS485 | Gigabit Ethernet, RS485; IP50 |

## Features

- **Synchronized RGB-D output:** Combines texture and geometry for object detection, localization, and grasping.
- **Real-time imaging:** Depending on the model, depth and RGB streams support up to 15–25 fps.
- **HDR imaging:** Supports high-contrast scenes and reflective surfaces.
- **Open development environment:** C/C++ and ROS SDKs support Windows, Linux, and Arm Linux.

## Key specifications

| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Laser | 940 nm VCSEL | 940 nm VCSEL | 940 nm VCSEL |
| Output | Depth map, RGB image, amplitude map | Depth map, RGB image, amplitude map | Depth map, RGB image, amplitude map |
| Depth resolution and frame rate | 640 × 480; up to 25 fps, typical 15 fps | 640 × 480; up to 15 fps, typical 12 fps | 640 × 480; up to 15 fps, typical 12 fps |
| Depth field of view | 81° × 61°, ±3° | 81° × 61°, ±3° | 81° × 61°, ±3° |
| RGB resolution and frame rate | 1280 × 960; up to 25 fps, typical 15 fps | 1920 × 1080; up to 15 fps, typical 12 fps | 1920 × 1080; up to 15 fps, typical 12 fps |
| RGB field of view | 86° × 55°, ±3° | 88° × 56°, ±3° | 88° × 56°, ±3° |
| RGB shutter | Global shutter | Rolling shutter | Rolling shutter |
| Operating range | 0.2–5 m | 0.3–5 m | 0.3–5 m |
| Ranging accuracy | ±3 mm + 0.25% × depth value | ±4 mm + 0.25% × depth value | ±4 mm + 0.25% × depth value |
| Average power consumption | 6.8 W (24 VDC) | 6.2 W (24 VDC) | 8.7 W (24 VDC) |
| Dimensions (L × W × H) | 92 × 47 × 51 mm | 88 × 47 × 47 mm | 95 × 80 × 41 mm |
| Weight | 449 g | 333 g | 428 g |
| Power supply | 24 VDC / 2 A | 24 VDC / 2 A | 24 VDC / 3 A |
| Communication interfaces | Gigabit Ethernet, CAN, four I/O channels | Gigabit Ethernet, CAN | Gigabit Ethernet, RS485 |
| Ingress protection | IP67 | IP42 | IP50 |
| Operating temperature | −20–60 °C | −20–60 °C | −20–60 °C |
