## Product overview

MRDVS M Series RGB-D depth cameras use iToF sensors to provide high-performance depth data from 0.2m to 5m. They output spatially and temporally aligned RGB and depth data for object recognition across different applications. The series includes M4 Mega, M4, and M4Pro models.

MRDVS also provides industrial solutions based on M Series depth cameras, including [smart forklift pallet recognition](/en/solutions/warehouse/pallet-recognition "Smart forklift pallet recognition"), [AGV forklift bin stacking](/en/solutions/warehouse/bin-stacking "AGV forklift bin stacking"), [3D vision-guided soft-package depalletizing](/en/solutions/warehouse/soft-package-depalletizing "3D vision-guided soft-package depalletizing"), and [3D vision-based volume measurement](/en/solutions/warehouse/volume-measurement "3D vision-based volume measurement") to simplify integration.

## Highlights

### RGB-D fusion improves recognition accuracy

M Series cameras combine color and texture from RGB images with ToF depth data to improve object-detection accuracy and robustness. In low light, complex backgrounds, or scenes with low color contrast, depth data provides the geometry needed to identify and locate objects accurately.

### High-performance real-time imaging

The series captures data at 15-25 frames per second for accurate measurement in dynamic environments, including package detection on conveyors and robotic-arm grasping.

### Operation in complex lighting

**HDR (high dynamic range)** captures both highly reflective and low-reflectivity objects for reliable operation in bright light or outdoors.

### Multi-platform support and development flexibility

The cameras support **C/C++, ROS, and Python** and run on Linux, Windows, and ARM Linux. Available tools and APIs support autonomous robots, AGV material handling, warehouse monitoring, and other applications.
