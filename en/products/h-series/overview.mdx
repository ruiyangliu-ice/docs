## Series overview

H Series industrial cameras are designed for high-precision recognition and picking tasks. They use binocular coded structured-light technology to deliver accurate 3D perception and real-time feedback in complex environments. Their performance and flexible integration options make them suitable for automated production, logistics, and robotic operations.
