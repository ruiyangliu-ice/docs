## Series overview

The MRDVS S Series is a line of RGB-D depth cameras designed for mobile robot obstacle avoidance. It captures 3D environment data and RGB texture in real time, including low and overhanging obstacles.

S Series cameras include obstacle-avoidance algorithms that combine RGB and depth images for semantic recognition and classification. They identify an obstacle's position, size, and type, such as a person or an object of a specific shape. Compared with 2D LiDAR, S Series cameras provide safer, more stable, and more intelligent obstacle avoidance for mobile robots.

The S10 Pro and S10 extend the S Series with real-time depth and texture capture for stable long-range perception on low-speed autonomous vehicles, autonomous forklifts, drones, and lawn mowers. Applications include obstacle avoidance, construction-equipment end detection, pile-hole measurement, intelligent transportation, warehouse logistics, and indoor or outdoor security.

## Series features

- **Multiple data interfaces:** Supports Ethernet. Custom interfaces are available.

- **RGB-D imaging:** Provides multimodal depth and RGB data.

- **Custom obstacle-avoidance zones:** Supports dynamic parameters for different zones, including warning and stop zones.

- **Semantic recognition:** Recognizes obstacles and supports intelligent decisions based on obstacle class.

- **Multi-camera interference resistance:** Supports multiple cameras operating together with minimal interference.

## Operating modes

- Send point cloud data from the sensor over Ethernet and integrate it into an existing obstacle-avoidance system.

- Use the built-in algorithm to configure dynamic 3D obstacle-avoidance zones and send commands to the controller through the port.
