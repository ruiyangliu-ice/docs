## Series overview

The S Series provides RGB-D perception for mobile robots and industrial automation equipment. Cameras can output depth maps, RGB images, and point clouds to detect low, overhanging, and conventional obstacles. Selected models also provide on-device obstacle-avoidance algorithms and I/O signal output.

Models cover short-range, high-precision sensing and medium- to long-range perception for mobile robots, autonomous forklifts, low-speed autonomous vehicles, drones, and lawn mowers. Refer to the specifications on each product page for model-specific capabilities.

## Core capabilities

- **Multimodal data output:** Depth, RGB, IR amplitude, and point-cloud data. Available data types and interfaces vary by model.
- **Integrated obstacle avoidance:** Connect point-cloud data to an upper-layer system, or configure obstacle-avoidance zones on supported models and output results through I/O.
- **Industrial deployment:** Designed for indoor and outdoor mobile scenarios. Refer to the model specifications for working range, environmental requirements, and protection rating.
- **Multi-camera deployment:** Supports coordinated use of multiple S Series cameras. Results depend on installation, scene reflectivity, and device configuration.
