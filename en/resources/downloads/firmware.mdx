<Info>
  Firmware downloads are being organized. To obtain or upgrade firmware, contact MRDVS technical support with the camera model, serial number, and current firmware version.
</Info>

## Before upgrading

- Confirm that the firmware matches the camera model and the current software and hardware versions.
- Keep the camera powered and the network connection stable during the upgrade.
- If the upgrade fails or the device cannot reconnect, keep the device logs and contact technical support.
