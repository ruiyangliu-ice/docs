> Company name: MRDVS Technology Co., Ltd.
>
> English name: Zhejiang MRDVS Technology

The MRDVS core technology team was established in 2016. It develops mobile robot vision sensors and integrated 3D vision hardware, software, and AI algorithms to help robots operate more safely, reliably, and intelligently.

The core members of the team have accumulated nearly 20 years of experience in the fields of optics, computer vision, etc. The company has comprehensive R&D capabilities in computer vision + mobile robots. Since its establishment, MRDVS has established strategic partnerships with many leading companies in the mobile robot industry, successfully delivered tens of thousands of 3D vision sensors, firmly positioned itself at the forefront of the industry, and led the development of mobile robot visual perception technology.

In the wave of rapid development of the mobile robot industry, MRDVS has become the leading provider of mobile robot vision solutions in China, and has won wide recognition in the industry with its outstanding technical strength and market performance. The company focuses on the field of mobile robot vision, taking innovation as its core driving force to continuously promote technological progress in the industry. At the same time, with the vision of "becoming a world-class mobile robot vision provider", it actively deploys the international market and develops a broader future.

## Technological innovation, industry leadership

MRDVS has the **exclusive top-view navigation technology**, which provides mobile robots with more accurate navigation and positioning capabilities, solves the limitations of traditional technology in complex environments, and enables robots to operate efficiently and stably in logistics warehousing, manufacturing workshops, commerce and other scenarios. As the mobile robot industry moves from indoor to outdoor and from low-speed to high-speed, MRDVS is keeping up with industry trends and continues to develop visual solutions that are smarter and better suited to scene needs, giving mobile robots new capabilities.

In the future, MRDVS's technological innovation will further empower the application of mobile robots in various scenarios such as **outdoor delivery vehicles**, **construction machinery vehicles**, **agricultural robots**, and **drone**. The company is also actively deploying visual technology with **semantic understanding capabilities** so that robots can not only "see" the world, but also "understand" the world, providing strong support for their intelligent operations in complex scenes.

## Win-win cooperation, market recognition

MRDVS has established long-term relationships with leading mobile robot companies. Its products support automated warehousing, intelligent logistics, industrial manufacturing, and other applications. Cumulative shipments have reached tens of thousands of units. The company is also expanding internationally with customers and partners worldwide.

## Moving towards internationalization, the vision is promising

As a benchmark enterprise in the field of domestic mobile robot vision, MRDVS continues to promote the internationalization of technology and services with the vision of "becoming a world-class mobile robot vision provider". The company has launched a global layout, extending its technological advantages to the international stage, and assisting the development of more industry fields through innovative visual solutions.

In the process of the mobile robot industry moving from **autonomous mobility** to **embodied intelligence**, MRDVS has always been at the forefront of the industry. Driven by technological innovation, it is committed to creating more adaptable, scalable and intelligent vision technology to promote mobile robots to broader application scenarios. The future of MRDVS has huge potential and is **limitless**.

## Company Development **Important Events**

### 2024

On December 12-13, 2024, MRDVS attended the 2024 (7th) Mobile Robot Industry Development Annual Conference and Humanoid Robot Scenario Application Ecological Summit, demonstrated the latest dToF depth camera product S10 Pro/S10, and delivered a keynote speech titled "3D Vision, Helping Mobile Robots Go from Indoor to Outdoor".

On November 19, 2024, MRDVS attended the 2024 World Internet Conference to demonstrate mobile robot 3D visual perception technology.

From July 4 to July 7, 2024, MRDVS attended the World Artificial Intelligence Conference (WAIC 2024) to demonstrate the company's application of 3D visual perception in the fields of obstacle avoidance, recognition and navigation of humanoid robots.

From May 15th to 17th, 2024, MRDVS will appear at the 5th China (South China) International Robot and Automation Exhibition (2024 IARS), demonstrating the cutting-edge technology of robot 3D vision to professional audiences at home and abroad.

On May 9, 2024, Zheng Weijun, general manager of MRDVS, was invited to attend the 13th Manufacturing Logistics and Warehousing Intelligent Engineering Conference and the 2nd Global Fork Mobile Robot Summit, and gave a keynote speech on "Empowering Mobile Robots with "Deep Vision"". In addition, MRDVS also presented 3D vision sensors and solutions dedicated to mobile robots.

On May 5, 2024, the VALSE 2024 Vision and Learning Young Scholars Seminar was held at Chongqing Yuelai International Conference Center, and MRDVS was invited to attend.

From April 23 to 26, 2024, MRDVS MRDVS appeared at the Seoul Logistics Industry Exhibition in South Korea, bringing four series of products: S (obstacle avoidance), M (identification and docking), V (navigation), and H (grasping), to demonstrate the 3D vision sensor + AI vision solution to world friends and robotics companies.

From April 9th to 10th, 2024, the first China Humanoid Robot Industry Conference and Embodied Intelligence Summit was held in Haidian District, Beijing. MRDVS was invited to attend the summit and won the LeadRobot2024 "Annual Humanoid Robot Industry Service Award".

From March 11 to 14, 2024, MRDVS will appear at the MODEX 2024 exhibition in the United States, demonstrating cutting-edge 3D vision technology and all-round sensing solutions for mobile robots to the global market, and releasing the high-precision ToF camera M4 Mega at the exhibition, which is deeply loved by overseas customers.

On January 15, 2024, MRDVS officially released the top-view navigation camera V1 Pro, which further improves navigation accuracy and ease of use, and helps significantly improve the product strength of mobile robots.

### 2023

From December 11 to 12, 2023, the finals of the 6th Huzhou Wuxing Global High-Level Talent Entrepreneurship Competition were held as scheduled in Wuxing, Huzhou, and the MRDVS project won the second prize in the finals.

On December 1, 2023, the 6th Huzhou Wuxing Global High-Level Talent Entrepreneurship Competition Suzhou City Competition was successfully held in Wuzhong District, Suzhou City, and MRDVS won the first prize.

In November 2023, MRDVS will appear at the 2023 iREX Tokyo International Robot Exhibition, the world's most influential robot exhibition, to explore the Japanese market.

In November 2023, the 2023 GaoGong Mobile Robot Annual Conference and GaoGong Golden Globe Award Ceremony was held in Hefei. MRDVS won the "Annual Innovative Technology Golden Globe Award".

In October 2023, MRDVS will appear at CeMAT ASIA 2023, the Asian international logistics technology and transportation system exhibition, attracting the attention of many robotics companies and professional audiences.

In October 2023, MRDVS's industrial-grade multi-modal camera S series developed for mobile robot obstacle avoidance applications was officially released. By obtaining the distance and texture information of obstacles in front, and combining it with AI algorithms to identify and classify obstacles, it improves the obstacle avoidance accuracy of mobile robots.

In September 2023, MRDVS will bring its full range of products and mobile robot 3D vision solutions to the 2023 China International Industrial Expo.

In August 2023, MRDVS will appear at TILOG-LOGISTIX 2023, a logistics equipment warehousing and transportation exhibition in Bangkok, Thailand, to explore the Thai market.

In July 2023, MRDVS M4 Pro industrial camera + intelligent algorithm was officially released to assist unmanned forklifts in realizing pallet docking and cage stacking.
