### Warehouse logistics automation application

[Case sharing: Automation upgrade of soft packaging depalletizing in a chemical company in Anhui](/en/solutions/warehouse/cases/chemical-soft-package-depalletizing)

A chemical company in Anhui used to rely on manual labor for depalletizing and handling soft package materials. Flexible packaging materials are easily deformed during the stacking process, and packaging materials are diverse, making manual depalletizing time-consuming and inefficient.

By adopting the **MRDVS 3D Vision Soft Packaging Depalletizing Solution**, 3D vision technology and intelligent algorithms are used to help the robotic arm accurately identify and grab soft packages, and transport them to the unloading port to automatically complete the unpacking operation.

### Commercial cleaning robot application

**Top-view SLAM+LiDAR fusion breaks through the limits of dynamic environments**

Faced with the challenges of shifting shelves and interweaving flows of people and vehicles in warehouses/factories, MRDVS takes **3D visual navigation** as the core and innovatively adopts the **top-view SLAM+2D lidar** solution—by capturing the stable features of the ceiling to achieve ±1cm precise positioning, get rid of the interference of ground changes, and successfully empower hundreds of commercial cleaning robots.

* * *

**3D vision + AI algorithm breaks through the bottleneck of agricultural automation**

The **multi-arm picking robot** developed by the **Intelligent Equipment Technology Research Center of the Beijing Academy of Agricultural and Forestry Sciences** can accurately identify fruits obscured by leaves and coordinate multiple robotic arms to complete efficient picking. And its "eyes" are the M-series RGB-D cameras of **MRDVS** - with the advantages of 6.0 Tops computing power on the end-side, RGB-D fusion perception, and high-density point cloud**, it has become an ideal visual solution for agricultural robots in complex outdoor environments.
