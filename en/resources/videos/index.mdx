### Yutong Technology smart workshop

Yutong Technology's smart workshop uses unmanned handling robots equipped with a 3D vision system:

- **Navigation**: Capture ceiling features with wide-angle vision for localization.
- **Obstacle avoidance**: Use semantic recognition to distinguish dynamic and static obstacles, then stop or detour as required.
- **Docking**: Support docking with pallets of different specifications.
