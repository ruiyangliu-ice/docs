In order to solve this problem, Maier Microvision has launched the **Intelligent Human Safety Detection Solution** to provide enterprises with a full range of personnel safety monitoring to ensure efficient production safety.

## Solution Overview

Maier Microvision's intelligent 3D human safety detection solution is based on self-developed M series 3D cameras and intelligent human body recognition software. It can monitor restricted areas in real time and accurately identify whether someone has crossed the boundary through AI algorithms. Once an abnormality is detected, the system will immediately issue an alert to help enterprises respond to potential security threats in a timely manner.

## System composition

The program mainly consists of the following two parts:

**1. M4 Pro 3D depth vision camera**:

a. It has powerful 3D visual capture capabilities and can accurately identify the dynamics of people in three-dimensional space.

b. **High-resolution imaging**: The camera provides a depth resolution of 640×480 and an RGB color resolution of 1920×1080, ensuring the clarity and richness of captured scene details.

c. **Precise RGB-D alignment**: The camera internally realizes precise alignment of RGB images and depth data in space and time, ensuring position synchronization and time consistency of multi-modal data.

b. The built-in algorithm does not require an external industrial computer, simplifying deployment.

## Solution advantages

**• High-precision detection**: The M4 Pro 3D camera can conduct all-round three-dimensional space monitoring and reduce false positives and negatives through accurate humanoid recognition technology.

**• Strong environmental adaptability**: The system can operate stably in a variety of lighting environments without additional light isolation processing, and can adapt to complex industrial environments.

**• Dynamic tuning**: Self-developed sensors can be dynamically tuned according to changes in monitoring scenarios to further improve the performance and stability of the system.

**• Low false detection rate and missed detection rate**: Under an ideal environment, the system can have a false detection rate as low as one in three million and a missed detection rate as low as one in one million and a half, ensuring the reliability of security monitoring (the test is conducted in a laboratory environment with an open field of view; the target person is standing, walking, facing each other, and facing away; the target person is not blocked; the target person is within 5 meters from the camera; the target person moves at a speed less than 3 m/s, no motion blur; good ambient light, etc. The above indicators are statistics of single-frame real-time monitoring results. The software supports turning on the filter algorithm for improvement. By adjusting the filter window size, the missed detection rate and false detection rate can be increased to one in 25 million).

## Working principle

**1. Continuous monitoring**: Capture dynamic images in the restricted area in real time, and intelligent human body recognition software analyzes the images and determines whether anyone enters the restricted area.

**2. Alarm trigger**: Once the system detects that a person has crossed the restricted area, the information will be immediately reported to the upper-level system and trigger the corresponding alarm and response mechanism.

**3. Feedback mechanism**: The system will continue to conduct testing until the security issues in the restricted area are resolved or the upper-level system terminates the task.

## Application scenarios

This solution is widely applicable to industrial production sites, especially workshops and factories with high safety requirements in restricted areas. Common application areas include:

• Automated production lines

• Warehousing and logistics area

• Dangerous goods storage area

• Special workshops or laboratories

## Notes

• The system is easy to install and occupies a small area. The M4 Pro 3D camera has built-in algorithms and requires no external equipment, reducing deployment complexity.

• It is recommended to perform regular maintenance on camera equipment, such as lens cleaning, to ensure long-term stable operation.

• The system is adaptable to various industrial environments and avoids use in severe dusty or extreme environments.
