Mobile robots operating in warehouses, hospitals, and service environments must detect both static and moving obstacles on their paths. 3D vision provides depth and RGB data for obstacle detection, classification, and avoidance planning.

## Perception limits of 2D LiDAR

2D LiDAR normally scans a single plane, which creates two limitations for obstacle avoidance:

- **No height information:** The scan can miss low or overhanging obstacles outside the scanning plane.
- **Limited scene information:** A planar contour does not describe an obstacle's full spatial shape or texture.
