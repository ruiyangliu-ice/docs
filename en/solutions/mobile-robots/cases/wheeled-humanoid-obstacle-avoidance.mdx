When a wheeled humanoid robot moves through a factory or warehouse, it must cover near-floor and surrounding obstacles. VersaBot mounts an MRDVS S3 RGB-D ToF camera at the bottom of the robot to capture environmental data near its travel path.

## Perception design

The S3 captures RGB images and depth data together, providing distance, color, and texture information. Mounting the camera low on the robot brings near-floor obstacles into the field of view and supplies 3D perception inputs to the higher-level avoidance policy.

## Application considerations

- **Near-floor coverage:** Detect small obstacles and objects at the edge of the travel path.
- **Dynamic environments:** Use perception data to identify people, carts, and other moving objects during operation.
- **Site adaptation:** Field of view, vehicle speed, obstacle material, lighting, and chassis vibration affect actual performance.
