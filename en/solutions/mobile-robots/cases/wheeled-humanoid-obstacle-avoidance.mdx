With its unique flexibility and stability, wheeled humanoid robots have gradually shown advantages in some specific scenarios. In order to ensure that the robot can move efficiently and safely in complex working environments, accurate detection of obstacles is crucial. The "Weiba" wheeled robot launched by Weimeier uses Meyer Microvision's RGB-D ToF camera S3, which is placed at the bottom of the robot to detect various obstacles in the surrounding environment. This configuration enables the Weibo robot to sense and avoid low and surrounding obstacles in real time, thereby ensuring safe driving in small and dynamically changing industrial environments.

The advantage of the Meyer Micro Vision S3 camera is that it has both RGB (color) and depth data acquisition functions. It can not only measure the distance of an object, but also capture its color and texture information. This provides the "Weibao" wheeled robot with rich environmental data, allowing it to more effectively identify and avoid low obstacles. Especially in complex environments such as factory workshops and logistics warehouses, the application of S3 cameras has greatly improved the robot's environmental perception capabilities and obstacle avoidance accuracy.

The S3 camera is designed to be installed at the bottom of the robot, allowing it to detect objects at lower heights when the robot is close to the ground. These objects are often difficult to capture with traditional 2D lidar or high-altitude cameras. Combined with the high-precision depth perception function of the S3 camera, the "Weibao" wheeled robot can accurately perceive low obstacles and respond quickly to avoid collisions.

The "Weibao" wheeled robot development team emphasized that the high frame rate and fast response capabilities of the S3 camera provide great advantages for the robot. Even during rapid movement, the S3 camera can still capture clear and accurate images of the environment, ensuring that the robot maintains a high degree of flexibility and safety when performing tasks. In some application scenarios that have extremely high requirements on time and efficiency, such as material transportation next to production lines or goods movement in warehouses, Weibo robots rely on real-time environmental data provided by S3 cameras to quickly make obstacle avoidance decisions, thereby greatly improving work efficiency.

In addition, the excellent performance of the S3 camera still performs well in handling complex lighting conditions. Whether it is a bright working area or a poorly lit corner, the S3 camera can provide stable environmental perception, ensuring that the "Weiba" wheeled robot can maintain a smooth working rhythm in various environments.
