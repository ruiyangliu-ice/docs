## Solution Overview

In modern industrial automation, precise positioning and navigation technology are core elements to improve production efficiency and ensure safe operation. Meyer Micro Vision's top-view positioning solution uses 3D vision technology, combined with advanced AI algorithms and hardware integration, to provide an efficient, flexible and reliable positioning method, which is especially suitable for navigation needs in complex environments.

Meyer MicroVision’s top-view navigation solution is based on a 3D vision camera, which is mounted on top of a mobile robot and positions itself by capturing the natural features of walls and ceilings. This system does not require markings on the ground or walls, and avoids the impact of changes in the ground environment on positioning stability. Combined with self-developed navigation algorithms, the system can provide accurate positioning and navigation services in dynamic and complex industrial environments.

## Core Advantages

**1. Natural positioning and wide-area coverage** Meyer Micro Vision’s top-view navigation system locates by identifying natural features on the ceiling and surroundings, getting rid of dependence on external markers such as reflectors, and greatly improves environmental adaptability and flexibility. The system has a wide measurement range of 1 to 12 meters and can adapt to complex industrial scenarios including high-rise rooftops, large open workshops, and floor-type warehouses. In workshops with frequent dynamic changes or warehouses with many objects blocking the system, the system can still maintain positioning stability and wide-area coverage capabilities.

**2. High accuracy and reliability** The system has built-in high-performance computing power, provides millisecond-level response, and supports repeated positioning accuracy up to ±1 cm, ensuring high-precision navigation and operations. This high-precision performance is particularly suitable for tasks that require precise operations such as automatic handling and precise docking. Even in complex environments where multiple vehicles are operating, it can still maintain stable navigation effects and a high degree of accuracy.

**3. Dynamic environment adaptability** The top-view navigation camera has strong light resistance and can work stably under 100Klux strong light conditions. It also supports high dynamic exposure mode and adapts to various lighting conditions. From direct sunlight to low-light lighting, the system can provide reliable environment perception and positioning services to ensure operational stability in complex environments.

> **Navigation Camera Hardware Highlights**

**1. Built-in computing power, simplified deployment** V2Pro is the industry's first navigation camera with built-in 6.0 Tops computing power. It has the ability to directly run navigation and positioning algorithms, getting rid of dependence on external industrial computers, greatly simplifying the installation process, and reducing equipment deployment and usage costs.

**2. Dual positioning to improve reliability** V2Pro integrates an inertial navigation unit (IMU) and combines 3D visual data to achieve dual positioning. Even when there is little environmental visual information, it can still ensure high-precision positioning, with repeated positioning accuracy up to ±1cm.

**3. Dual network port design to improve compatibility** The camera is equipped with dual network ports, which can connect AGV and lidar at the same time, improving the compatibility and stability of the integrated system and reducing network resource conflicts.

> **Software Highlights**

**1. Automatic mapping, easy to operate** The upper computer software of the top-view navigation system has an automatic mapping function. By scanning environmental features with the camera, maps can be quickly generated and support subsequent map management, switching and uploading, greatly simplifying the deployment process.

**2. Multiple development interfaces** V2Pro provides rich SDK, API and ROS topic interfaces to facilitate users' flexible integration, greatly improves development efficiency and system compatibility, and meets different development needs.

## Application scenarios

**1. Warehousing and logistics** In large warehousing and logistics centers, especially in environments with frequently changing layouts, V2Pro can effectively avoid occlusion problems when multiple AGVs run at the same time, ensuring the stability of positioning and the accuracy of multi-vehicle operation.

**2. Manufacturing** The demand for automated transportation in manufacturing factories is increasing day by day. With its high precision and environmental adaptability, the top-view navigation solution helps companies improve the efficiency of intelligent logistics handling and promote the smooth operation of production lines.

**3. Complex warehousing environment** For floor-type warehouses, corridors or high-rise roofs, the V2Pro system locates by identifying the natural features of the ceiling, which is especially suitable for unmanned operations in large areas and complex environments.

## Workflow

**1. Camera installation and connection** Install the V2Pro camera on the mobile device and establish connections with devices such as lidar and odometer.

**2. Environmental mapping and mapping** The camera starts to scan the environmental data, generates a visual map and imports it into the system as the basis for subsequent navigation.

**3. Real-time positioning and navigation** During the operation, the camera captures the characteristics of the ceiling and surrounding environment to provide precise positioning and navigation of the equipment in real time.
