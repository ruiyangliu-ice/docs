In modern industry and logistics, accurate volume measurement has become a key requirement to improve operational efficiency and optimize resource management. Traditional volume measurement methods often have problems such as long time consumption, large errors, and high labor costs, and cannot meet the high requirements of enterprises for accuracy and efficiency. The introduction of 3D volume measurement solutions can significantly improve the accuracy and speed of measurement and reduce operating costs.

> **Common non-contact volume measurement solutions**

- Laser profile measuring instrument is a line laser scanning camera. Its working principle is to measure the profile size of a section within a specific time. When using a laser profiler to obtain object volume data, the camera or object needs to be moved at a constant speed for continuous scanning, so it is not suitable for static measurement.

- Scene light curtain volume measurement equipment calculates the volume based on light curtain occlusion, and its accuracy is relatively limited when measuring complex shapes.

- 3D vision measurement equipment combines laser measurement and 3D vision technology to generate high-precision models through three-dimensional reconstruction algorithms, adapt to complex environments, and provide efficient and accurate measurement solutions.

## Deployment Notes

* * *

Environmental requirements: Although the equipment has good dynamic adaptability, optical imaging may be affected in scenes with severe dust, excessive oil pollution, or heavy water mist. It is recommended to ensure that the measurement environment is clean.

Installation requirements: Ensure that the bracket of the M4 Pro series camera has sufficient rigidity and stability, which is an important prerequisite for ensuring measurement accuracy.

Applicable object types: This solution is suitable for complex-shaped objects such as special-shaped parts, highly reflective parts, and bulk cargo. It is especially suitable for industrial scenarios that require high-precision volume measurement.
