title: "3D vision-guided soft-package depalletizing"
description: "Use an RGB-D camera and depalletizing algorithm to identify irregular bags, cartons, and sacks and provide grasp positions and order to a robot arm."
---

Soft packages, cartons, and sacks can deform, adhere to one another, or sit at an angle, making recognition, grasping, and sequence planning difficult for a robot arm. This solution targets automated depalletizing of plastic pellets, fertilizer, food products, and similar materials.

## Solution overview

An RGB-D camera captures color and point-cloud data from the stack. A camera-side algorithm identifies target packages and their positions. The result can generate a grasp sequence and provide the calibration data needed for robot-arm coordinate conversion.

## Calibration and integration

The system provides a four-point calibration method to calculate the transformation matrix between the camera and robot arm. It has been adapted to industrial robot brands including ABB, KUKA, FANUC, AUBO, and DOBOT and supports TCP/IP communication. Confirm the actual integration scope through project validation.
