Company F is a leader in China's optical communications field. In recent years, as the wave of Industry 4.0 and smart manufacturing sweeps across the manufacturing industry, Company F has achieved significant improvements in production and logistics efficiency through the application of large-scale automation equipment in its digital smart factory. The unmanned stacker forklift assumes key internal logistics tasks and is responsible for the precise handling and docking of pallets and cable trays.

In Company F's smart factory, the angles and positions of pallets and cable trays are often irregular, which poses a great challenge to the accurate identification and docking capabilities of unmanned stacker forklifts. In addition, the logistics environment inside the factory is complex, and the equipment needs to operate in dynamically changing scenarios. It must avoid low obstacles and ensure safe operation when the forklift is as high as 2.2 meters, so as to ensure smooth and efficient logistics operations.

In order to solve these complex logistics challenges, F Company's unmanned stacker forklift deployed Meyer Microvision's pallet recognition system, 3D visual obstacle avoidance solution and V series top-view navigation system to ensure the accuracy and safety of each handling operation.

## 1. Tray and cable tray identification

Through MyerMicrovision's PalletPro pallet identification system, unmanned stacker forklifts can capture the depth information of pallets and cable reels in real time, ensuring that even if the pallet is placed in an irregular position, its position and attitude can still be accurately identified. When the stacker forklift approaches the pallet by about 200mm, the system will automatically adjust its position to ensure accurate docking between the pallet and the stacker forklift. Through this system, the stacker forklift completes 500 docking, handling and stacking tasks every day, greatly improving the overall handling efficiency.

## 2. 3D visual obstacle avoidance solution

Company F's stacker forklifts and lurking AGVs both adopt Meyer Microvision's 3D visual obstacle avoidance solution. Whether it is a low lurking AGV or a 2.2-meter-high stacker forklift, the system can effectively identify low or suspended obstacles and maintain stable operation of the equipment in complex scenarios. Through 3D vision, the equipment can be adjusted in real time in a dynamic environment to ensure safe and smooth handling. Whether in narrow aisles or in open storage areas, stacker forklifts can perform tasks efficiently.

## 3. Top view navigation system

To ensure accurate navigation, F Company's stacker forklifts are equipped with Meyer MicroVision's V series top-view navigation system. The system achieves high-precision positioning by capturing the natural features of ceilings and walls, ensuring that equipment can maintain stable operation despite the complex layout inside the factory. The top-view navigation system is not only suitable for complex scenarios in which multiple vehicles operate in parallel, but also has high-precision positioning capabilities. On average, each stacker forklift travels nearly 10 kilometers per day, and the cumulative journey of 18 stacker forklifts reaches 50,000 kilometers, seamlessly supporting all-weather logistics operations.

By deploying Meyer Micro Vision's pallet recognition, 3D visual obstacle avoidance and top-view navigation systems, Company F has achieved a comprehensive upgrade of logistics in its smart factory:

**1. Efficient and accurate handling operations:** The pallet recognition system of the unmanned stacker forklift ensures the accuracy of docking and handling tasks. Even when the pallets are placed in irregular positions, it can still complete 500 handling and stacking operations per day.

**2. Improved safety and stability:** The 3D visual obstacle avoidance solution provides all-round environmental perception capabilities for stacker forklifts and latent vehicles, allowing the vehicles to flexibly avoid obstacles in complex environments and ensure operational safety.

**3. Stable all-weather logistics support:** The V series top-view navigation system ensures the stable operation of multiple vehicles in large-scale factories. Each stacker forklift travels nearly 10 kilometers per day, supporting efficient all-weather logistics operations.
