## Project background

Jushi Group built a smart manufacturing base in Tongxiang. Its internal logistics system uses custom metal pallets that must be handled by unmanned forklifts. The site contains **3–4** types of non-standard pallet, and pallets can become misaligned during transport.

## Project challenges

- **Multiple pallet types:** Custom metal pallets have substantially different geometries, making coverage by conventional recognition methods difficult.
- **Misalignment correction:** The unmanned forklift needs the relative pose of the pallet and load to adjust its fork angle.

## Solution

Bluebotics deployed an unmanned forklift equipped with MRDVS **PalletPro**. The system uses a 3D ToF camera to capture depth data and detect pallet position and pose, providing inputs for autonomous correction and fork pickup.

## Results

The solution supports recognition and automated handling of multiple custom metal pallets, reducing the effect of pallet shape and placement deviations on the pickup process.
