China Jushi Group is the leader in the global fiberglass industry, with its annual fiberglass production capacity ranking first in the world. In 2017, Jushi Group deployed an intelligent manufacturing base in Tongxiang, with the goal of building an unmanned factory, promoting the construction of glass fiber electronic cloth intelligent factories and realizing digital, networked and intelligent production. The pallets used by <br /> Jushi Group’s internal logistics are all customized metal pallets. The special pallet design brings huge challenges to logistics automation. In order to improve the efficiency of automated handling, Jushi hopes to solve the shortcomings of the existing system in pallet identification and handling efficiency by introducing Blue Core Technology's unmanned forklifts, and achieve intelligent handling throughout the entire process.

## Difficulties of the project

1. **Difficulty in pallet identification**: Jushi Factory’s production line uses 3-4 types of special-shaped customized metal pallets. These pallets have different shapes. Conventional laser recognition or non-intelligent vision solutions are difficult to meet the high-precision requirements of the automation system for pallet detection, affecting the operational safety and accuracy of unmanned forklifts.

2. **High difficulty in forking**: Pallets often deviate during transportation. Unmanned forklifts need to have accurate identification of the position and posture information of the pallet and goods, and can independently adjust the angle to complete precise forking and transportation operations.

## Solution

Blue Core Technology has deployed an unmanned forklift solution based on an intelligent vision system for China Jushi, equipped with Meyer Microvision's **PalletPro** pallet recognition system. The system provides high-precision depth data through a 3D ToF camera to detect the specific position and posture of the pallet in real time, ensuring that the unmanned forklift can accurately identify and autonomously correct deviations. Combined with Blue Core Technology's unmanned forklift technology, the system can handle a variety of pallet shapes and placement deviations, achieving fully automated and efficient pallet handling.

## Results display

This solution successfully solved the problem of identifying and forking special-shaped pallets in Jushi Group's internal logistics, significantly improved handling efficiency and system stability, and helped the smooth advancement of Jushi Group's intelligent manufacturing base. Through the combination of unmanned forklifts and intelligent vision technology, Jushi has further realized the fully automated handling process of the production line from raw materials to finished products, reducing errors and costs caused by manual operations, and significantly improving the operational efficiency of the factory.
