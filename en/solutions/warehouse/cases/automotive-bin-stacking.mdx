## Project background

In the logistics center of an automobile manufacturing plant, automated stacking management of cages containing stamped parts is required. Each cage has a single layer height of 1.6 meters and needs to be stacked in four layers, with a total height of 6.4 meters. The weight of stamped parts loaded in each cage exceeds 1 ton, so this project places extremely high requirements on **high-precision docking** and safety during the stacking process.

## Solution

MRDVS provided a **3D vision bin-stacking solution** for this project. An M4 Pro camera installed on the smart forklift detects the relative pose of each cage and guides the forklift during high-precision stacking. The system monitors and adjusts position and angle in real time to keep the **maximum gap between cages below 5 mm** and ensure accurate, safe stacking.

> **Key Technology**

**• Multi-layer stacking**: The system supports four-layer stacking of single-layer 1.6-meter-high cages, with a total stacking height of 6.4 meters.

**• High-precision docking**: The relative posture of the forklift and the material cage is calculated in real time through the 3D camera to ensure that the identification error of the material cage feet during the stacking process is less than 5mm, ensuring stacking stability.

**• High weight load**: The solution can safely handle cages weighing more than 1 ton, and the docking accuracy is controlled at the millimeter level to ensure safe stacking under high loads.

**• Safety protection and alarms**: The system monitors the stacking process for abnormal conditions. If the detected pose deviation exceeds the safe range, the forklift backs away and repeats detection and docking. The system can also issue an **abnormal-condition alarm** so the operator can intervene before a hazardous situation develops.

## Results display

Through Meyer Microvision's 3D vision basket stacking solution, the logistics center of the automobile manufacturer has realized an efficient and safe automated stacking process. During the stacking process of each layer of cages, the system ensures millimeter-level precision and safely and stably completes multi-layer stacking, improving storage efficiency and effectively reducing manual intervention.
