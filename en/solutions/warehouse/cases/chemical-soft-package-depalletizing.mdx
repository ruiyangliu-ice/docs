## Project background

As a large chemical company, a chemical company in Anhui used to rely on manual labor for depalletizing and handling of soft package materials. Flexible packaging materials are easily deformed during the stacking process, and the packaging materials are diverse. Manual depalletizing is not only time-consuming and inefficient, but also faces high safety risks, such as package dropping and breakage.

## Solution

In order to cope with these challenges, the company adopted the **Myer Micro Vision 3D Vision Soft Packaging Depalletizing Solution**, which uses 3D vision technology and intelligent algorithms to help the robotic arm accurately identify and grab soft packages, and transport them to the unloading port to automatically complete the unpacking operation and enter the next step.

## Plan implementation

1. **Visual recognition and automated grabbing** <br /> Myer Micro Vision's soft package depalletizing system uses a high-performance RGB-D camera combined with an AI algorithm to capture the surface texture and three-dimensional point cloud data of soft packages in real time. It can identify the deformation and stacking position of irregular objects, ensuring that the robotic arm can complete grabbing stably and accurately.

2. **Integration of robotic arms and automated production lines** The <br /> system automatically plans the grabbing sequence of the robotic arms, safely and efficiently transports chemical soft bags to the unloading port for unpacking operations, and seamlessly connects subsequent processes, comprehensively improving the level of automation.

3. **Anti-light environment interference and real-time data processing** The <br /> system has excellent anti-ambient light interference performance and can ensure stable visual recognition capabilities regardless of strong or weak light environments. In addition, the depalletizing algorithm runs in real time inside the camera, which greatly improves the system response speed and operating efficiency.

## Results display

1. **Efficiency is significantly improved**<br /> Through Meyer MicroVision's soft packaging depalletizing solution, the company's automated depalletizing efficiency has been significantly improved, and the overall production line operation has become smoother.

2. **Significant cost reduction** <br /> Due to the reduction of manual participation, the company's labor costs are reduced, and at the same time, the problems of package dropping and broken packages caused by improper manual operations are avoided.

3. **Safety Improvement** <br /> automated depalletizing not only reduces safety hazards in manual operations, but also improves the reliability and consistency of the overall operation and reduces the probability of production accidents.

Through the Myer MicroVision soft packaging depalletizing solution, a chemical company in Anhui achieved an upgrade from manual depalletizing to intelligent automated depalletizing. This solution not only helps enterprises improve production efficiency and reduce operating costs, but also greatly improves security, becoming an important driving force for enterprises' intelligent upgrades.
