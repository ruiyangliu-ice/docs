Smart forklifts need a pallet's spatial position and pose to dock during pickup and placement. Lighting, background, pallet geometry, and stacking density can affect recognition based on 2D images or single-point ranging.

</Frame>

## Solution overview

PalletPro uses a ToF depth camera to capture a pallet point cloud and outputs geometric features, position, and pose through the recognition algorithm. Historical test records report distance error within **±10 mm** at a **2 m** working distance; validate actual performance against pallet type, installation, and site conditions.

## Solution characteristics

- **3D pose output:** Support pallet recognition from initial localization through fine positioning.
- **Multiple pallet types:** Configure recognition for pallets with different shapes, sizes, and materials.
- **Complex-scene recognition:** Use depth data to reduce the impact of complex backgrounds and lighting changes.
- **Outdoor support:** Historical material includes outdoor tests; validate the target environment before deployment.
