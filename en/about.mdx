## Technology and application focus

MRDVS develops products and technologies around the perception needs of mobile robots:

- **3D visual perception**: Capture depth, point-cloud, and RGB data for obstacle detection, object recognition, and spatial measurement.
- **Visual localization and navigation**: Use natural top-view features for mapping, localization, and navigation in warehouses, manufacturing facilities, and other indoor environments.
- **Scene algorithms and integration**: Provide algorithms and interfaces for pallet recognition, cage stacking, volume measurement, and personnel-entry detection.

Our products are used in automated warehousing, intelligent logistics, and industrial manufacturing. Results depend on sensor selection, installation, site conditions, and system integration; validate the system at the target site.
